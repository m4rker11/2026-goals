# Week 1: Seattle Baseline

**Focus:** Establish baseline while traveling
**One behavior:** Do 2 short workouts + PT stretches
**Success metric:** 60+ minutes total

---

## The Principle

You're traveling. The goal isn't to crush it - it's to **not lose momentum**.

Two short sessions + PT stretches proves you can work out anywhere. That's the win.

---

## The Sessions

### Session 1: Upper (Bodyweight)
**When:** Day 2-3 in Seattle
**Duration:** 20 min
**Location:** Brother's house

```
Warmup (3 min)
- Arm circles, shoulder rolls

Pushups: 3 sets × max (write down the number)
Rows: 3 sets × 10 (use a sturdy table edge or heavy bag)
Dips: 2 sets × 8 (use a chair)
Plank: 2 × 20 sec

Cool down (2 min)
```

### Session 2: Mid (PT Focus)
**When:** Day 5-6 in Seattle
**Duration:** 15 min
**Location:** Anywhere

```
Dead bugs: 3 sets × 10 each side
Bird dogs: 3 sets × 10 each side
Glute bridges: 2 sets × 12
Cat-cow: 2 sets × 10
Child's pose: 1 min hold
```

### PT Stretches
**When:** 2 times during the week
**Duration:** 15 min each

Your regular PT routine. These count toward minutes.

## If You Miss a Session

**Don't spiral.** You're traveling. One session + PT stretches is still a win.

If you miss Upper:
- Do it the next day, or
- Swap for a 15-min bodyweight circuit

If you miss Mid:
- Just do PT stretches - they cover similar ground
