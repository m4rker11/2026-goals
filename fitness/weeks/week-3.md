# Week 3: Return + First Swim

**Focus:** Back in NYC, add swimming
**One behavior:** 2 gym sessions + 1 swim + PT stretches
**Success metric:** 130+ minutes total

---

## The Principle

You're back in NYC with full gym access. Time to **add a new modality**.

Swimming is cardio that's easy on joints. You said you want to do it. This week, you do it.

---

## The Sessions

### Session 1: Upper (Gym)
**When:** Day after return
**Duration:** 30 min
**Location:** Friend's building gym

```
Warmup (5 min)
- 3 min light cardio (bike or treadmill)
- 2 min arm circles, band pull-aparts

Pushups: 4 sets × max
Pullups: 3 sets × max (note the number)
Dumbbell rows: 3 sets × 10 each arm
Shoulder press: 2 sets × 10
Dips: 2 sets × 8
Bicep curls: 2 sets × 12

Cool down (2 min)
```

### Session 2: Lower (Gym)
**When:** 2 days after Upper
**Duration:** 25 min
**Location:** Friend's building gym

```
Warmup (3 min)
- Leg swings, bodyweight squats

Goblet squats: 3 sets × 12
Lunges: 3 sets × 10 each leg
Romanian deadlifts: 3 sets × 10
Calf raises: 3 sets × 15

Stretch (3 min)
```

### Session 3: Swim
**When:** Any day
**Duration:** 45 min (including changing)
**Location:** YMCA

```
Actual swim time: ~30 min
- Freestyle laps, rest as needed
- Don't worry about speed
- Just get in the water and move

Goal: Complete the session without quitting early
```

### PT Stretches
**When:** 2 times during the week
**Duration:** 15 min each

## If You Miss a Session

**If you skip the swim:**
- This is the main goal of the week. Try to reschedule.
- If you truly can't, replace with 30 min cardio at gym

**If you skip Lower:**
- That's the pattern. Notice it, don't shame it.
- Make sure Upper and Swim happen.

---

## Swim Logistics

**YMCA prep:**
- [ ] Know which YMCA location
- [ ] Check lap swim hours
- [ ] Bring: swimsuit, goggles, towel, lock
- [ ] Budget: ~$15-20 per visit (or check day pass options)

**First time anxiety:**
- It's normal to feel awkward
- Other people are focused on themselves
- Slow laps are still laps
