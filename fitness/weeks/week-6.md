## The Sessions

### Session 1: Upper - Push
**Duration:** 35 min
**Location:** Gym

```
Warmup (5 min)

Pushups: 4 sets × max (you should be improving)
Dips: 3 sets × 10
Shoulder press: 3 sets × 10
Chest fly: 2 sets × 12
Tricep work: 2 sets × 12

Cool down (2 min)
```

### Session 2: Upper - Pull
**Duration:** 35 min
**Location:** Gym

```
Warmup (5 min)

Pullups: 4 sets × max
Dumbbell rows: 3 sets × 12 each
Face pulls: 3 sets × 15
Lat pulldown: 2 sets × 12
Bicep curls: 3 sets × 12

Cool down (2 min)
```

### Session 3: Cardio (Zone 2)
**Duration:** 25 min
**Location:** Gym

```
Pick one:

- Bike: 25 min steady
- Incline treadmill: 25 min

Zone 2 = can hold a conversation
This builds your VO2 max base
```
