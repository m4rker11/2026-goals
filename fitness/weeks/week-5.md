# Week 5: Bollywood Begins

**Focus:** Integrate Bollywood dance into routine
**One behavior:** 2 gym sessions + Bollywood + PT
**Success metric:** 155+ minutes total

---

## The Principle

Bollywood dance is 60 minutes of cardio you'll actually enjoy.

This week, it becomes part of your routine. **Enjoyment is underrated.**

---

## The Sessions

### Session 1: Upper (Gym)
**Duration:** 35 min
**Location:** Gym

```
Warmup (5 min)

Pushups: 4 sets × max
Pullups: 4 sets × max
Dumbbell rows: 3 sets × 10 each
Shoulder press: 3 sets × 10
Dips: 3 sets × 10
Bicep curls: 2 sets × 12

Cool down (2 min)
```

### Session 2: Lower (Gym)
**Duration:** 30 min
**Location:** Gym

```
Warmup (5 min)

Goblet squats: 4 sets × 12
Romanian deadlifts: 3 sets × 10
Lunges: 3 sets × 10 each
Leg press (if available): 2 sets × 12
Calf raises: 3 sets × 15

Stretch (3 min)
```

### Session 3: Bollywood Dance
**Duration:** 60 min
**Location:** Dance studio (ClassPass or direct)

```
Just show up and dance.
- It's cardio
- It's coordination
- It's fun
```

## Bollywood Logistics

- Class booked (where? what time?)
- Know how to get there
- Comfortable clothes that let you move
- Water bottle

**First class tips:**
- Stand in the back if you're nervous
- Nobody cares if you mess up
- Focus on having fun, not perfection

---

## If You Miss a Session

**Bollywood is the anchor this week.** Prioritize it.

If gym sessions slip:
- Do 1 Upper session minimum
- Lower can wait (you're still building toward it)
