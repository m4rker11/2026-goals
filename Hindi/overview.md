## The System

**Two-track approach:** Grammar chapters + tutor practice

| Track | Purpose | Weekly Task |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Chapters | Systematic grammar reference | Read synopses, understand patterns |
| Mohit | Practice sentences, drilling | Extract to Anki after session |
| Nahid | Conversation practice | Send chapter/topic beforehand |
| Anki | Retention | 3 review sessions per week |

---

## Tutors

| Tutor | Focus | Format |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **Mohit** | Grammar drilling, practice sentences | Lots of example sentences, exceptions |
| **Nahid** | Conversation, topic practice | You choose what to practice |

**Workflow:**
1. Mohit session → extract sentences to Anki
2. Pick chapter for Nahid → send before session
3. Nahid session → practice speaking
4. Anki reviews → retain everything
