**Date:** January 11, 2026

## Session Overview

Structured lesson on requests, permissions, and suggestions in Hindi. Covered four main constructions: polite requests (-इए), suggestions/warnings (-ना), permission seeking (-ऊं/-एं), and "let me" constructions. Also touched on auxiliary verbs and ऐसे/वैसे/जैसे patterns.

---

## Topics Covered

### 1. Time Expressions (Review)

| Situation | Construction | Example |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| Exact hour | बज रहे हैं | पांच बज रहे हैं |
| Hour + minutes | बजकर... हो रहे हैं | पांच बजकर सात minute हो रहे हैं |

**Key insight:** Once you add minutes, use बजकर (not बज रहे) because the "रहे" is already implied in "हो रहे हैं".

---

### 2. Polite Requests (-इए Ending)

**Rule:** Add -इए to the verb stem for polite requests (please do X).

| Verb | Request Form | Meaning |
|------|--------------|---------|
| खाना | खाइए | Please eat |
| जाना | जाइए | Please go |
| रखना | रखिए | Please keep/place |
| सुनना | सुनिए | Please listen |

**Exceptions (use -ीजिए):**
| Verb | Request Form |
|------|--------------|
| देना | दीजिए |
| लेना | लीजिए |
| पीना | पीजिए |
| करना | कीजिए |

**Negative requests:** Use मत + verb + -इए
- मत देखिए = Don't look
- मत छुइए = Don't touch
- मत पूछिए = Don't ask

**Toning down formality:** Replace -इए with -ओ
- खाइए → खाओ
- जाइए → जाओ
- Exception: दीजिए → दो, लीजिए → लो

---

### 3. ऐसे/वैसे/जैसे/कैसे Pattern

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| ऐसे | like this | ऐसे कीजिए (do like this) |
| वैसे | like that | वैसे कीजिए (do like that) |
| जैसे | like what/as | जैसे मैं करता हूं (as I do) |
| कैसे | like how/how | कैसे करना है? (how to do?) |

**Pattern:** जैसे... वैसे = "as I do... do like that"

---

### 4. Suggestions & Warnings (-ना Ending)

**Rule:** Use infinitive form (-ना) for suggestions/advice (not immediate).

| Request (immediate) | Suggestion (later) |
|--------------------|--------------------|
| खाइए | खाना |
| मत करिए | मत करना |

**Key difference:**
- Requests = urgency, do it now
- Suggestions = relaxed, do it later/in general

**Examples:**
- भारत में ऐसे मत करना = Don't do like this in India (general advice)
- भारत में ऐसे बात मत करना = Don't talk like this in India
- दिल्ली में गाड़ी मत चलाना = Don't drive a car in Delhi
- गहरे पानी में मत जाना = Don't go in deep water
- अजनबियों से बात मत करना = Don't talk to strangers

**Restaurant context:** Use -ना (suggestion) even when ordering, to avoid sounding like royalty demanding service.

---

### 5. Permission - "Should I?" (-ऊं Ending)

**Rule:** Add -ऊं for "Should I / May I / Can I" (I is implied).

| Verb | Permission Form | Meaning |
|------|-----------------|---------|
| करना | करूं? | Should I do? |
| खाना | खाऊं? | Should I eat? |
| जाना | जाऊं? | Should I go? |
| देखना | देखूं? | Should I watch? |

**Examples:**
- ऐसे करूं? = Should I do like this?
- मैं उससे बात करूं? = Should I talk to him/her?
- मैं आपकी मदद करूं? = Should I help you?

**"Should we?" uses -एं:**
| Verb | We Form | Meaning |
|------|---------|---------|
| करना | करें? | Should we do? |
| जाना | जाएं? | Should we go? |
| शुरू करना | शुरू करें? | Should we start? |

**Examples:**
- हम शुरू करें? = Should we start?
- यह वाला try करें? = Should we try this one?

---

### 6. Two Verbs in One Sentence

When you have purpose + movement (go to eat, go to study):

**Pattern:** [purpose verb]-ने + जाना

| English | Hindi |
|---------|-------|
| Should I go to eat? | खाने जाऊं? |
| Should I go to study? | पढ़ने जाऊं? |
| Should I go to roam? | घूमने जाऊं? |

**Why -ने?** के लिए (for) is implied, and के लिए triggers oblique case.

---

### 7. "Let Me Do" Construction

**Pattern:** मुझको + [verb]-ने + दीजिए

| English | Hindi |
|---------|-------|
| Let me go | मुझको जाने दीजिए |
| Let me eat | मुझको खाने दीजिए |
| Let me speak to him | मुझको उससे बात करने दीजिए |
| Let me eat in peace | मुझको चैन से खाने दीजिए |

**Less formal versions:**
- दीजिए → दो (casual)
- दीजिए → दे (informal)

**For others:**
- उसको आने दीजिए = Let him/her come
- उनको मेरे साथ आने दीजिए = Let them come with me

---

### 8. वाला/वाली - Extended Uses

| Use Case | Example | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| This/that one | यह वाला, वो वाला | this one, that one |
| Origin/location | America वाली aunt | my American aunt |
| Profession | दूध वाला, चाय वाला | milkman, tea seller |
| Above | ऊपर वाला | God (the one above) |

**Gender agreement:** Match the noun being modified
- America वाला uncle (m)
- America वाली aunt (f)
- वो वाले try करें (m pl - if referring to masculine noun)

---

### 9. Auxiliary Verbs (Introduction)

Three common auxiliary verbs: ले, दे, जा

**Don't change meaning, add subtle nuance:**

| Without | With | Nuance |
|---------|------|--------|
| देखूं? | देख लूं? | Should I watch (if you don't mind)? |
| सोइए | सो जाइए | Go to sleep (completeness) |

**Key point:** Which auxiliary goes with which verb must be memorized - they're selective.

## Practice Sentences from Class

### Requests (-इए)
- ऐसे कीजिए = Do like this
- ऐसे मत कीजिए = Don't do like this
- अंदर आइए = Come inside
- बाहर मत जाइए = Don't go outside
- मेरी मदद कीजिए = Help me
- मेरा इंतज़ार कीजिए = Wait for me
- मुझसे बात मत कीजिए = Don't talk to me
- मेरा सामान मेरे कमरे में रखिए = Place my belongings in my room

### Suggestions (-ना)
- भारत में ऐसे मत करना = Don't do like this in India
- वहां जाना = Go there (later)
- दिल्ली में गाड़ी मत चलाना = Don't drive in Delhi
- अपना काम करना और किसी से बात मत करना = Mind your business and don't talk to anyone

### Permission (-ऊं/-एं)
- ऐसे करूं? = Should I do like this?
- मैं जाऊं? = Should I go?
- हम शुरू करें? = Should we start?
- मैं थोड़ी देर TV देखूं? = Should I watch TV for a little while?

### Let Me (मुझको... दीजिए)
- मुझको जाने दीजिए = Let me go
- मुझको चैन से खाने दीजिए = Let me eat in peace
- मुझको उससे बात करने दीजिए = Let me talk to him/her

- **Driving in Delhi:** Don't do it as a foreigner. "People die not because of their mistake, but other people's mistakes on the roads."
- **Swimming:** Most Indians don't know how to swim (no water bodies around). First thing Indians learn when moving West.

## Key Takeaways

1. **-इए** = polite request (do it now)
2. **-ना** = suggestion/warning (do it later/in general)
3. **-ऊं** = Should I? (permission for self)
4. **-एं** = Should we? (permission for group)
5. **मुझको + verb-ने + दीजिए** = Let me do X
6. Auxiliary verbs (ले, दे, जा) add nuance but don't change core meaning
