# Unit 6: Present Tense - Imperfective

## Key Concepts
- **Imperfective present**: Describes habits/regular actions
  - Formation: stem + -taa/-te/-tii + auxiliary (hoon, ho, hai, hain)
  - "main boltaa hoon" (I speak - m), "main boltii hoon" (I speak - f)
  - "ham bolte hain" (we speak - m.pl)
  - Participle agrees with subject gender/number
- **Negative drops auxiliary**: "main sigreT kabhii nahiin piitaa" (I never smoke)
  - Feminine plural negative: participle nasalized -tiin (ve nahiin piitiin)
- **Extended uses**:
  - Ongoing states of mind: "vah sochtaa hai ki..." (he thinks that...)
  - Immediate future: "acchaa, main caltii hoon" (well, I'm off)
  - Time expressions: "kitne baje" (at what time) + imperfective
- **Language ability with aanaa**: "hamein hindii aatii hai" (we know Hindi - lit: Hindi comes to us)
- **General truths with hotaa hai**: vs specific hai
  - "acchii saaRiyaan mahangii hotii hain" = good saris ARE expensive (general)
  - "yah saaRii sastii hai" = this sari is cheap (specific)
- **Naming with kahnaa**: "is lipii ko kya kahte hain?" = What's this script called?
- **Possessive pronouns**: meraa/tumhaaraa/aapkaa/uskaa - agree with possessed noun
- **apnaa (one's own)**: Reflexive - used when possessor = subject
  - "main apnii kitaab paRhtaa hoon" = I read MY (own) book
  - NOT used when possessive is part of subject: "meraa bhaaii lambaa hai"
  - In commands, 'your' = apnaa: "apnii kitaab paRho"
- **Compound postpositions**: ke/kii + word (ke lie = for, ke baare men = about)
