## Prep Checklist (Per Item)

Before Feb 1, each item needs:

- [ ] **Collect** - Item is in one place, accessible
- [ ] **Clean** - Dust off, wipe down
- [ ] **Test** - Verify it works (or note defects)
- [ ] **Photograph** - 3-5 good photos, natural light
- [ ] **Research** - Check eBay sold listings for price
- [ ] **Write listing** - Title, description, specs

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Best For | Shipping? |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **eBay** | Tech items, niche items, national reach | Yes |
| **FB Marketplace** | Large items, local pickup, quick sales | No (local) |

**Rule of thumb:**
- Small + shippable → eBay
- Large + heavy → FB Marketplace (local pickup)
