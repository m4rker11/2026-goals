# Chapter 23: Relative-Correlative Clauses (Jo-Vo)

**Goal:** Complex Sentence Architecture.

---

## 23A. Person & Thing

### 23A-i. Jo... Vo

> **Example:** "Jo mehnat karta hai, vo safal hota hai"
> *(He who works hard, succeeds.)*

---

### 23A-ii. Jise/Jisko (Oblique)

> **Example:** "Jis ladke ko tumne dekha, vo mera bhai hai."
> *(The boy whom you saw, he is my brother.)*

**Key Difference:** *Jo* becomes *Jis* because of *Ko* (oblique case transformation).

---

### 23A-iii. Jo (Thing)

> **Example:** "Jo kitab mez par hai..."
> *(The book that is on the table...)*

---

## 23B. Time & Place

### 23B-i. Jab... Tab (When... Then)

> **Example:** "Jab main pahuncha, tab barish ho rahi thi."
> *(When I arrived, then it was raining.)*

---

### 23B-ii. Jahan... Vahan (Where... There)

> **Example:** "Jahan tum ho, vahan main hoon."
> *(Where you are, there I am.)*

---

### 23B-iii. Jidhar... Udhar (Whither/Direction)

> **Example:** "Jidhar dekho, udhar pani hai"
> *(Wherever you look, there is water.)*

---

## 23C. Quantity & Manner

### 23C-i. Jitna... Utna (As much as...)

> **Example:** "Jitna chaho, utna lo."
> *(Take as much as you want.)*

---

### 23C-ii. Jaisa... Vaisa (As... So)

> **Example:** "Jaisa karoge, vaisa bharoge"
> *(As you do, so shall you reap.)*

---

### 23C-iii. Jaise hi... Vaise hi (As soon as...)

> **Example:** "Jaise hi vo aaya, main chala gaya."
> *(As soon as he came, I left.)*

---

## Quick Reference: Relative-Correlative Pairs

| Relative | Correlative | Meaning |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Jo | Vo | Who/Which... That |
| Jab | Tab | When... Then |
| Jahan | Vahan | Where... There |
| Jidhar | Udhar | Whither... Thither |
| Jitna | Utna | As much... So much |
| Jaisa | Vaisa | As... So |
| Jaise hi | Vaise hi | As soon as... |
